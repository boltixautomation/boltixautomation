Our Secure Acquisition Process

We are committed to a transparent, simple, and secure process for acquiring Boltixautomation.com. Below are the steps involved.

Step 1: Choose Your Purchase Method

You have two primary options on our homepage. The "BUY NOW" button will take you to a secure checkout page on GoDaddy, a world-leading domain registrar. The "MAKE AN OFFER" button allows you to contact us directly to negotiate a price or discuss terms.

Step 2: Secure Payment

All transactions are handled through trusted, industry-standard platforms. If you use the "Buy Now" option, GoDaddy's secure payment system will process the transaction, offering you full buyer protection. For private sales, we can use a trusted escrow service to ensure funds are held securely until the transfer is complete.

Step 3: The Domain Transfer

Once payment is confirmed, we will initiate the domain transfer. This is a simple process. We will provide you with an EPP (Extensible Provisioning Protocol) or "Auth Code." You will provide this code to your preferred registrar to begin the transfer. If you use GoDaddy, we can simply "push" the domain to your account, which is nearly instantaneous.

Step 4: Post-Purchase Support

Our commitment doesn't end after payment. We will provide full support and guidance until the domain is confirmed to be safely and securely in your account. We are available to answer any questions you may have during the transfer process.
